Construct your stratifications for the plots. The first textarea contains a collection of phenotype id, sex, and ancestry inputs to be plotted. It is initally empty, and you may add more inputs by filling out the input boxes as needed, and pressing the Add button. Finally, press Submit to see your plots! Do note that manhattan plot only supports 1 or 2 inputs, while qq and pca plot supports more than 2 inputs.
